About Me

Hi There!
I've had the fortune of being alive for 23 incredible years now and I've picked up a lot of skills along the way.(I make some of the best tomato rice this side of the Atlantic, can't beat my Amma though :D).

An avid reader, I look at the world through a lens that's coloured by the thoughts and ruminations of some of the greatest minds from the East and the West. My parents, Vyasa, Valmiki, Krishna, Vivekananada, Maha Periyava on one side and my parents,Steven Erikson, Asimov, Clarke, Newton, Einstein, Feynman and the rest on the other. Yeah, my parents are on both sides depending on their mood. Can never thank them enough :D.
Though I have a Bachelor's Degree in Instrumentation and Control from the best National Institute of Technology in India, NIT-Tiruchirappalli and a Masters in Electrical Engineering from North Carolina State University, I've always been more of a generalist and believe strongly in lifelong learning and pushing the boundaries of what's possible.
So,if you're looking to talk to someone about any of the following: Instrumentation, Control Systems, Electrical Engineering, Computer Vision, Deep Learning, Machine Learning, Computer Graphics, Virtual Reality, Robotics or any other topic under the sun, you've come to the right place!
